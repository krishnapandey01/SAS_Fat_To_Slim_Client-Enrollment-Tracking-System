About Shikha Aggarwal Sharma

Shikha Aggarwal Sharma is a Dietitian and Nutritionist specializing in Indian based diet. She is the head of Fat To Slim, a diet plan company with multiple centers in India and abroad. With a client base of 30000+ clients all across the globe, Shikha is known for her revolutionary approach to weight loss and healthy living.

Unlike other dietitians who focus on restrictive diets and fancy salads, Shikha's diet is all about Indian food and putting nutrients in the body. Her concept of 'Eat & Sleep' has helped thousands of clients lose up to 7 kgs and 4 inches from various parts of their body within one month, without exercises and without making them starve.
